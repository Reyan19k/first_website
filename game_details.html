Apex Legends:

Free-to-play, Battle royale shooter game.


Apex legend is a free to play battle royale game, while its structure is very familiar to other battle royale games. in terms of dropping into a large map, picking up random loot that is scattered around the map and fighting isnide the circle that keeps getting smaller. However, the aspect that makes its vastly different to any other battle royale, is its characters called legends. There are 21 legends as of the 2nd of may, all legends have 3 different abbilities, a passive, tactical and ultimate. This makes every in game incounter so unique, as u dont know what legend your going to face, when you do have incounter your actions are always different for each legend. Another aspect of apex legends is its heavy prioritisation on team work, your in a team of 2 or 3 players. There are also different game mode, first game mode is the normal battle royale, second is the rankend where people play a more commpetitive version of the game and thirdly the area mode, which is a 3v3 game mode that is more casual and new player friendly.

, ,

Online tacticle shooter game.


Rainbow Six Siege is a First Person Shooter (FPS). Players compete in multiple game modes, such as rescuing a hostage, defusing a bomb or securing a designated area. Teams of five attack or defend, while choosing from 54 different characters, called operators. A unique aspect of rainbow six siege is its use of terrain, mainly that the terain is destructable. This allows the players to devise different ways to enter a buiding or even a room, to gain an edge over the apposing team. It encourages safe and tactiple engagements, and has an emersive game play that makes your in game life precious.
